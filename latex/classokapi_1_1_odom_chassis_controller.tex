\doxylink{classokapi_1_1_odometry}{Odometry} based chassis controller. Starts task at the default for odometry when constructed, which calls {\ttfamily \doxylink{classokapi_1_1_odometry_a00d4dd37fe02d4f02fc8896a2a760331}{Odometry::\+step}} every {\ttfamily 10ms}. The default State\+Mode is {\ttfamily State\+Mode::\+\+FRAME\+\_\+\+TRANSFORMATION}.

Moves the robot around in the odom frame. Instead of telling the robot to drive forward or turn some amount, you instead tell it to drive to a specific point on the field or turn to a specific angle relative to its starting position.
